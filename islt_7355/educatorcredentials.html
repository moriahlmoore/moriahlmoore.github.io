A little about me...

My name is Moriah and I am an archivist and librarian in a local history collection on Long Island, New York actively pursuing a Masters Degree in Library and Information Sciences. As a long time taphophile, I indulge my interest in graves and grave recordings with frequent visits to cemeteries from the small and close at hand to the massive and famous. Some of my favorite cemeteries I have visited are
Sleepy Hollow Cemetery in Sleepy Hollow, NY
Pere Lachaise Cemetery in Paris, France
Highgate Cemetery in London, England
Woodlawn Cemetery in Bronx, NY
The cemetery at San Miniato Al Monte in Florence, Italy
The Granary Burying Ground in Boston, MA
And the Jewish Cemetery on the Mount of Olives in Jerusalem, Israel
In addition to the library with which I currently work, I have also worked in an archival role at an academic library and as a researcher and the interim archivist at the Shelter Island Historical Society.
