Resources

Below are several resources referenced in Let's Learn as well as some links to additional resources you may find interesting, such as the website for the Association for Gravestone Studies.

The University of Tennessee on the yucca plant and it's use as a grave marker.
#plantofthemonth: Yucca: McClung Museum of Natural History & Culture.
McClung Museum of Natural History & Culture | University of Tennessee, Knoxville. (2022, April 20).
The website for the Association for Gravestone Studies, the academic and practical authority on American graves post-colinization.
Association for Gravestone Studies, Greenfield, MA. (n.d.).
An academic article published in Southeastern Archaeology surveying two South Carolinan cemeteries for African Americans, focusing on above-ground features exclusively. Note: Article may be behind a paywall.
Brooks, C. (2011). ENCLOSING THEIR IMMORTAL SOULS: A SURVEY OF TWO AFRICAN AMERICAN CEMETERIES IN GEORGETOWN, SOUTH CAROLINA.
Southeastern Archaeology, 30(1), 176-186.
A PDF provided by The Thomas Wolfe Memorial website that goes into detail on additional gravestone symbols and the meansing associated with them.
Gravestone symbols and their meanings - wolfe memorial. (n.d.).
The UK Funeral Guide website with grave symbols illustrated and translated. These images are used on the Let's Learn page.
Gravestone symbols: Grave & cemetery symbolism. Funeral Guide. (n.d.).
A ground penetrating radar survey of a public park, revealing graves hidden beneath. Image used on the Let's Learn page.
Historic graves located in a modern City Park GPR survey: Scanning. sensoft. (n.d.).
Funeral Help Center provides textual instructions for tombstone cleaning.
Raymond, C. (2021, September 20). How to clean a cemetery tombstone or marker. Funeral Help Center.
The U.S. Department of the Interior offers instructions for tombstone cleaning.
U.S. Department of the Interior. (n.d.). Cleaning grave markers (U.S. National Park Service).
National Parks Service.
A YouTube video from the Millenial Stone Cleaner giving instructions for tombstone cleaning.
YouTube. (2022, August 9). How to clean a headstone - cleaning headstones explained. YouTube.
